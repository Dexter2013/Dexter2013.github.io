Jalapeno Basil Gin & Tonic

  • Time: 5 Minutes
  • Serves: 1
Quantity Ingredients
1 Jalapeno, sliced
2 Sprigs Fresh Basil
1 Small Cucumber, sliced
2 oz Gin
Tonic

To make: Muddle jalapeño, basil, and cucumber in the bottom of a cocktail shaker. Fill shaker with ice, add gin Opens a New Window. , and shake vigorously. Strain cocktail into ice-filled glass and top off with tonic before serving. For a more impressive looking drink, layer a couple slices of jalapeño, a cucumber slice, and a sprig of basil between the ice in your glass before pouring the drink.

Cucumber Thai Chili Lemonade

  • Time: 10 Min
  • Serves: 6
Quantity Ingredients
1-2 Cucumbers, sliced
1 cup Lemon Juice
1/4-1/2 cup Agave syrup
1/2 Small thai chili
3 cups Water
1 cup Vodka

To make: Add lemon juice, agave, Thai chili, water, and all but 6 of the cucumber Opens a New Window. slices to a blender and process till smooth. Strain. Stir ingredients in a pitcher filled with ice. Serve, adding a cucumber slice to each finished drink.

Pineapple Cilantro Serrano Cocktail

  • Time: 5 Min
  • Serves: 1
Quantity Ingredients
1-2 Sprigs fresh cilantro
2-3 Wedges fresh lime
1 Serrano pepper, thinly sliced
2oz Tequila
3 oz Pineapple juice
To taste Agave nectar

To make: Muddle cilantro, lime, and Serrano pepper in a cocktail shaker. Fill shaker with ice, add tequila Opens a New Window. and pineapple juice, and shake vigorously. Strain into ice-filled glass.
